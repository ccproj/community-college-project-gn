Community College project

The Community College project aims to open the door of opportunities at Google, that, up until now, have only been offered to undergraduate (4-year colleges) and graduate students. Now it is time for Community College Students to be considered for these opportunities! Let's open the door to a talented and diverse community of students. Learn more about LaGuardia and its high-achieving students. Lead a community college student in his/her journey to Google through a video game.Google hires people who are smart and determined recognizing potential and ability.Our students aim high, work hard and push themselves beyond the limit.
